Installation Guide

Android Users

  1. Visit the download page: Download Pokémon Unite.
  2. Click the orange button to download the game.

iPhone Users

  1. Change App Store Country/Region to China Mainland:
    • Open the Settings app on your iPhone.
    • Tap on your Apple ID at the top (your name).
    • Select Media & Purchases.
    • Tap View Account and authenticate if prompted.
    • Tap Country/Region.
    • Select Change Country or Region.
    • Choose China from the list.
    • Agree to the terms and conditions.
  2. Set Up Payment Method:
    • Select None as it is not required for this free game.
  3. Enter Address Information:
    • Street: You can enter any address.
    • Postcode: Enter 100000.
    • Region: Choose any region.
    • Province: Select the first province in the list.
    • Phone: Enter 12345678901.
  4. Download WeChat:
    • WeChat is required for verification. Download it from the App Store.
    • Note: WeChat may be banned in some countries. If you encounter issues, consider using a VPN. Some popular options include NordVPN, ExpressVPN, or CyberGhost.
  5. WeChat Verification:
    • Open Pokémon Unite and click the green WeChat Login button.
    • Follow the prompts to verify using WeChat.
Note: To play the tft/Autochess mode, you need to be at least level 7 so play other game modes to reach this.
