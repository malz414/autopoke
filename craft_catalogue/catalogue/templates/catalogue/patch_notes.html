Hologrid Battle (Beta) Adjustments

Patch Notes - May 14

Core Gameplay Adjustments

  • Reduced the amount of move gauge recovery after taking damage.

Pokémon Projection Adjustments

Unless otherwise specified, the following adjustments apply to all evolution stages of the mentioned Pokémon projections.

Trevenant
  • Horn Leech: HP recovery reduced (30% → 15%).
Urshifu
  • Basic attack speed: 7000 → 9000
  • Unite Move (Multi-Strike): Damage per hit increased for second and third stages (70% → 85% of Attack).
Clefable
  • Moonlight: Healing ratio reduced by 15% (135%/135%/405% → 115%/115%/345%).
Beartic
  • Unite Move: Shield ratio reduced by 20% (1.5% → 1.2%).

[Pokémon Synergy Adjustments]

Grass
  • ×6: Reduced Grass Terrain HP recovery (5% → 4%).
Fairy
  • Zacian: Unite Move damage reduced (700% → 600%).
Fire
  • ×6: Duration of low-HP buff effect reduced (5s → 3s).
Poison
  • ×3: Damage boost effect increased (30% → 50%).
  • ×4: Damage boost effect increased (100% → 150%).

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed an issue in Hologrid Warfare (Beta) where Azumarill, Hoopa, and Tyranitar could abnormally use their special moves by exploiting discs.
  • Fixed an issue where Beartic's ice pillars incorrectly contributed to the Grass-type synergy's Verdant Seed points.

Patch Notes - May 8

Hologrid Battle (Beta): Arena Customization & Free Giveaway

The new season introduces arena customization! The first decoration, "Abyssal Magma", is free via a seasonal event after the update goes live.

Major Gameplay Update

Hologrid Battle (Beta) adds 4 new team combinations and 9 new Pokémon Projections for expanded strategic options.

New Team Combinations

    • If undamaged for 3s, enter “Ghost” state (boosts Attack, Sp. Atk, Attack Speed, damage reduction).
    • X2: +15% Atk Speed/Sp. Atk, 40% Damage Reduction
    • X3: +25% Atk Speed/Sp. Atk, 40% Damage Reduction
    • X4: Ghost state auto-triggers every 5s; immune to interruption
  • Flying
    • X2: Wind barrier every 6s for 20% damage reduction (3s)

  • Poison
    • X2: +30% Poison DoT
    • X3: +100% Poison DoT
    • X4: Toxic status to random enemy every 2s (160/s)
    • X5: Higher Toxic frequency; poison becomes fixed damage
  • Steel
    • X2: Lucario and Tinkaton immune to hindrance effects
