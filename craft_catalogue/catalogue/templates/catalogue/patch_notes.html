Patch Notes - April 2

Combination Adjustments
  • Guardian
    • Defense & Sp. Defense: 60/120/240 → 90/180/360
  • Eevee
    • [×4]: Basic attacks now grant 6 move gauge charge (previously 4).
  • Grass Type
    • HP recovery effect increased: 1%/2%/3%/4%/5% → 2%/3%/4%/5%/6%
  • Fire Type
    • [×5]: Burn damage on all enemies reduced: 5% max HP/sec → 3.5% max HP/sec
  • Fighting Type
    • HP steal ratio adjusted at 2/3/4/5/6 synergies: 12%/25%/40%/60%/60% → 10%/20%/30%/45%/45%
  • Dark Type
    • Bonus attack scaling per Fear stack reduced: 5%/10%/20%/30%/60% → 5%/10%/18%/28%/40%
  • Psychic Type
    • [×6]: Move damage bonus 40% → 30%
Pokémon Projection Adjustments

Unless otherwise specified, the following adjustments apply to all stages of the respective Pokémon projections.

  • Cramorant
    • Hurricane: Damage increased (150% → 180%).
  • Alolan Ninetales
    • Dazzling Gleam: Damage increased (170% → 200%).
    • Aurora Veil: Bonus damage within the field increased (20% → 25%).
