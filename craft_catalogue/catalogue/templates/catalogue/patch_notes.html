Hologrid Battle (Beta) Adjustments

Patch Notes - March 5

Gameplay Adjustments
  • Removed the setting where players cannot see their next opponent.
Combination Adjustments
  • Fighting Type
    • Increased attack speed: 5%/10%/15% → 10%/15%/20%
    • [X5]: Additional damage from normal attacks: 260% → 280%
  • Ice Type
    • [X4]: Increased Defense and Special Defense: 60 → 120
  • Psychic Type
    • Increased Move Progress per second: 2/4/6/8 → 4/6/8/10
  • Dark Type
    • [X5]: Reduced Defense and Special Defense reduction: 60% + 120 → 50% + 120
Pokémon Projection Adjustments

Unless otherwise specified, the following adjustments apply to all stages of the respective Pokémon projections.

  • Pikachu
    • Thunderbolt damage: 280% → 330%
  • Lapras
    • Unite Move cooldown: 8 seconds → 6 seconds
  • Mamoswine
    • Unite Move shield: 24% HP → 30% HP
  • Crustle
    • Unite Move cooldown: 8 seconds → 7 seconds
  • Blastoise
    • Hydro Pump immobilize time: 1 second → 1.5 seconds
  • Leafeon
    • Razor Leaf damage: 24% → 30%
    • Leaf Blade damage: 260% → 280%
  • Delphox
    • Fire Spin damage: 150% Special Attack → 180% Special Attack
  • Urshifu
    • Unite Move: Reduced additional attack speed per hit: 10% → 8%
    • Unite Move: Duration extended by 50% when hindered after activation.
