What is Pokémon Auto Chess?

Pokémon Auto Chess is a strategy-based auto battler similar to Teamfight Tactics (TFT). Players assemble teams of Pokémon, each with unique abilities, and battle automatically on a grid-based board. The key to victory lies in understanding Pokémon synergies, positioning, and item optimization.

Read More

How to Play

  1. Build Your Team: Select Pokémon from a rotating shop and form a team with strong synergies.
  2. Positioning: Place Pokémon strategically on the board to maximize their abilities.
  3. Equip Items: Enhance your Pokémon's strength with items found throughout the match.
  4. Battle Rounds: Pokémon automatically fight enemy teams, and the winner deals damage to the opponent.
  5. Adapt & Strategize: Keep up with the changing meta, upgrade Pokémon, and adjust your strategy to win.
